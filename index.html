Musical Ear Simon

Press PLAY to start for a random tune musical Simon or Play Tune for a specific tune Simon.

Hint: You can play the notes before starting the game.

  1. Click Play, recognise and replicate note
  2. Repeat
  3. Don't forget that in every turn you have to replicate ALL notes played so far.
  4. You can press the Repeat button if you want to listen to the note again!
